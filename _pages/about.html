Hi, my name is Brad Willner and I am a local artist dabbling in many things including video games, comic books, animation and toy design.

I grew up in Bothell, Washington where I now live with a family of my own. I have been in love with toys since childhood, a love that evolved into my primary passion and sparked my desire to become a professional toy maker. As a stay at home father to two young boys for the past 4 years, I've spent thousands of hours refining my craft and honing my skills as an artist. Being a father has taught me to be patient, caring, steady and dependable. As much as I love being home with my boys, I'm ready to apply my skills to a career I can have fun with and throw myself into.

I have been practicing Zbrush for a few years, focusing primarily on character design. I recently invested in my first 3D printer and have been practicing my hand at the entire toy-making process, from inception of the character concept to the sculpting of the model to the printing and subsequent refinement of the finished product. I use a Flash Forge printer that prints using PLA, and it has been a great tool to begin my journey into 3D printing.

My immediate goal is to get hired on as a professional artist to create the toys of tomorrow. I have always been inspired to create and self-motivated to continue to improve my skills and become a better artist. I really want a opportunity to work with great artists who will, by proxy, pull the best out of me. I work well with others and I love to learn from those around me and am very humble in my process to take feedback and critiques of my work.
